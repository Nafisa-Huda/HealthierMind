HealthyMind

A mindfulness-based app that takes kids ages 8-12 on an interactive journey to learn about mental wellbeing and how fun it can be to create a healthier mind.

HealthyMind is a safe space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences without judgment. Healthymind offers different kinds of tools to help you process these thoughts and feelings such as, beginners meditation techniques, breathing exercises, fun activities designed to calm the mind and resources on how you can improve your self-care.
