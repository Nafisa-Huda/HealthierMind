About Us

HealthierMind is a safe space for you to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences without judgment. Our goal was to build a space for kids who wanted to improve their well-being in a fun and interactive way. Being mindful does not have to boring or difficult. Rather there's many ways to begin and this friendly guide is a great way to start.

HealthierMind offers different kinds of tools to help you process these thoughts and feelings such as, beginners meditation techniques, breathing exercises, fun activities designed to calm the mind and resources on how you can improve your self-care. This mindful journey will change your life! Don't forget to share HealthierMind with your friends, family and classmates!
